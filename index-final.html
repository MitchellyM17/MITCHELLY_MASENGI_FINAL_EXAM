STUDENT

A student or learner is an individual who is actively involved in the learning process, either in a formal environment such as school, college, university, or in special training. They have the main goal of gaining knowledge, skills, and experience that will support their personal or professional development in the future. As learners, they are required to be diligent, disciplined, and have a high curiosity about various things.

TEACHER

A teacher is an individual who plays a vital role in the educational journey of others by imparting knowledge, skills, and values.

CLASSROOM

A classroom is a dedicated space where teaching and learning take place.
